    • Membership & Visit

    We welcome drop-ins for tours and offer tiered memberships for frequent users. Members receive access to advanced equipment and discounted classes.

    Getting Started

    Sign up for an orientation to learn shop rules, safety procedures, and how to reserve equipment — orientations run weekly.

    Hours & Location

    Open Tue-Sat, 10:00 AM-8:00 PM. Located at 88 Forge Lane, downtown — free street parking after 6 PM and bike racks at the front door.

    Volunteer & Teach

    Interested in teaching a class or volunteering? We partner with experienced makers to expand offerings and mentor new members.
